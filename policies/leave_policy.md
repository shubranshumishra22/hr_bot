# Leave Policy (v3)

## Leave types
- **Earned leave (EL)**: accrues at 1.5 days per completed month, credited at the start of each month.
- **Sick leave (SL)**: fixed allocation of 6 days per calendar year, credited every January 1st. Unused sick leave does not carry forward.
- **Casual leave (CL)**: fixed allocation of 6 days per calendar year for short, unplanned absences. Maximum 2 consecutive CL days without manager pre-approval.

## Carry forward
Up to 10 unused earned leave days may be carried forward into the next calendar year. Any earned leave beyond 10 days as of December 31st is automatically encashed at the employee's basic daily pay rate. Sick leave and casual leave do not carry forward and lapse at year end.

## Approval process
All leave requests must be submitted at least 2 working days in advance, except sick leave, which can be applied for on the day of absence. Leave requests route to the employee's direct manager for approval. Leave exceeding 5 consecutive working days additionally requires HRBP sign-off.

## Blackout periods
Leave requests during the last week of each fiscal quarter require additional manager justification due to close-related workload, but are not blocked outright.

## Maternity and paternity leave
Maternity leave is 26 weeks paid, per statutory requirement. Paternity leave is 2 weeks paid, to be availed within 3 months of the child's birth. Both require HR notification at least 4 weeks in advance where possible.
