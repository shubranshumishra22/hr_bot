# Work-From-Home Policy

## Eligibility
All full-time employees past their probation period (90 days) are eligible for hybrid work arrangements, subject to manager approval based on role requirements.

## Standard arrangement
Employees may work from home up to 2 days per week without prior notice, provided core collaboration hours (10am-4pm local time) are maintained and calendars are kept up to date.

## Extended remote work
Requests for more than 2 WFH days per week, or fully remote arrangements, must be submitted to the employee's manager and HRBP at least 1 week in advance, and are evaluated on a case-by-case basis considering role and team needs.

## Equipment
The company provides a laptop and a one-time home-office setup allowance. Requests for additional equipment go through the IT ticketing system, not HR.
